Privacy Policy

CapitalsQuiz - Geography Learning App
Effective Date: December 28, 2025
TL;DR: CapitalsQuiz does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your quiz data stays on your device.

Introduction

Welcome to CapitalsQuiz. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. CapitalsQuiz is designed with privacy as a priority:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No advertising or third-party SDKs
  • No network connections (the app works completely offline)

Data Storage

All quiz data, including your progress, statistics, and preferences, is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Is protected by your device's security measures
  • Is deleted if you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

CapitalsQuiz does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app is completely self-contained.

iCloud Sync (If Applicable)

If the app uses iCloud sync in the future, your data would be synced through your personal iCloud account, encrypted and managed by Apple. We would not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

CapitalsQuiz is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there are no privacy concerns regarding children's use of the app.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your data security is managed by your device's built-in security features and your personal security practices.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any of your personal data:

  • There is no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers
  • You have complete control over your local data
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this privacy policy and notify users through the App Store update notes. The "Effective Date" at the top of this policy will be updated accordingly.

Open Source

CapitalsQuiz is open source. You can review the code to verify our privacy practices at: github.com/dlevi326/CapitalsQuiz
